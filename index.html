Privacy & Data Safety

WorthNot is designed with a "Local First" architecture.

  • Your financial SMS and bank notifications are processed locally on your device.
  • We do NOT upload, store, or share your transaction data, messages, or personal information to any external servers or third parties.
  • The app requires Notification Access and SMS permissions solely to automate spending detection for your own reflection.

Disclaimer

WorthNot is a spending awareness and reflection tool. It does not provide financial advice. The "alternative value" calculations are for educational and motivational purposes only. We are not responsible for any financial decisions made based on the app's data. Always consult a professional for financial planning.
